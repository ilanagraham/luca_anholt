My name is Luca Anholt and I go by the producer name Kutaka.

I've been writing and producing music for around 6 years, but I'm keen to grow, learn and collaborate.

Having been raised in a multicultural family in London, my musical influences are very varied, especially because my parents often played music from around the world when I was younger.

I like to challenge myself by producing and writing music from many genres and I'm always excited to try something new and find new influences.
